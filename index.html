Jagmohan Sharma is a science graduate and has always been keen to take up opportunities that allow him to add value to the lives of others – mostly old age homes and orphanages. Having accomplished several milestones in corporate and education travel for almost a decade, his love for social work continues to grow. Being a part of the founding team at Explorars has further enabled him to ignite and inspire young minds. As a Program Director at Explorars, he has found immense inspiration and satisfaction. It has provided opportunities for outdoor learning with students on programs that encapsulate life transforming objectives. Preparation, planning and experience has helped him make key decisions on the ground. Jagmohan’s spirit and work ethic has had led him to work with reputed institutions like Harvard Business School, Pathways Group of Schools, GD Goenka, Delhi Public School and Delhi Private School Dubai.

Jagmohan Sharma

Pracheta Jaitely is a Delhi based photographer who finished her Masters in photography design from the National Institute of Design, Ahmedabad. Born and brought up in Delhi, she did her schooling from Holy Child Auxilium and then decided to take up English Honours for her undergraduate study. After completing her graduation in literature from Delhi University, she worked with the World Bank for one year and hosted on-air shows at All India Radio before she finally joined NID. A die-hard adventure sports lover, she loves to travel and explore. She takes pleasure in working in an analog medium. Her projects are mostly an amalgamation of literature and photography, through which she expresses herself and brings out her creative potential.
